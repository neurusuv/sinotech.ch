报告人嘉宾简介/Invited speakers

 

马欢博士

Dr. Huan Ma

Huan Ma is presently a Post-doctoral fellow in the Swiss Federal Laboratories for Materials Science and Technology (Empa), working on thin film research. He has obtained his Ph.D. from ETH Zurich, following the Master’s degree from RWTH Aachen University and Bachelor degree from University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB). From July, He will start his new position as applied scientist at Bruker. Now, HuanMa is the secretary general of the Association.

 

吴大林博士

Dr. Dalin Wu

Dalin Wu is presently a Post- doctoral fellow in the university of Basel, after he obtained his Ph.D. degree. Dalin got his Bachelor and Master’s degree from Zhengzhou University. Later, he will join Department of Biomedical Engineering in Sun Yat- sen University (SYSU) as associate professor, independent PI.

 

阙再青博士

Dr. Zaiqing Que

Zaiqing Que is working in Paul Scherrer Institute and will join the Technical Research Centre of Finland (VTT) as research scientist (permanent), with focus on “Corrosion and Fracture of Metal Materials”. After finishing his Bachelor degree and Master’s degree from University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB) and RWTH Aachen University, respectively, he obtained his Ph.D. in École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL).
